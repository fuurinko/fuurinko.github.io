6.3.3 Detaching Computation

Sometimes we want to just focus on specific varibles. For example, suppose we have z = x * y and y = x * x , but we want to focus on the _direct_ influence of x on z rather than the influence conveyed via y.
So we create a new varible u = x * x, but u has no ancestors in the graph, and gradients do not flow through u to x.
We can consideru as a _constant_ with the value of x * x.
